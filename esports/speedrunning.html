Speedrunning X-treme

What is speedrunning?

Speedrunning is the act of completing a game as fast as is possible. There are many different categories of speedruns. Each game's speedrunning community will choose one of these categories as the main category, but runners are free to run which ever they want. The categories are:

  • With Gliches: Use every glich that makes the run faster.
  • No Major Gliches: Some gliches are not allowed, as decided by the community.
  • Glichless: No gliches allowed.
  • Any%: Only goal is to get to the end creddits.
  • 100%: Must complete all of the game in order to be in this category.
  • Low%: Complete as little of the game as is possible.

Why is Speedrunning Fun?

Speedrunning is a way for people to keep being challenged by their favourite games even after they have beaten them dozens of times. For many speedrunning is a challenge of their dexterity, patience, and wits, as they race their own best time, or even the world record. For others, speedrunning is about breaking apart a game, seeing what makes it tick. These people will use tools such as frame inputs to find gliches or exploits. These people are called TASers (Tool Assisted Speedrunners)
