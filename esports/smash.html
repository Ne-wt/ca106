Super Smash Brothers X-treme

What is Smash?

Super Smash Brothers is a platform fighter series developed by Nintendo. The lead creator is Masahiro Sakurai. The aim of the game is to knock one's opponent(s) off the screen into an area called the "blast zone." When this happens, the player who was knocked out loses a stock. A stock is like a life. When you have 0 stocks, you lose. In most competitive play, 3 stocks are used.

How to Play

As mentioned previously, the aim of the game is to knock the opponent into the blast zone. In order to do this, players must attack each other. There are 4 types of attacks: tilts, arials, smashes, and specials. Every attack can be combined with a directional input for a different attack. Eg: up-arial, forward-smash, neutral-special. There is also a grab, which can be used to throw opponents, and a shield which blocks attacks, but not grabs. Every time a player gets hit, they take percentage. Higher percentage means you get knocked back further, making it easier to knock you into the blast zone.
