Super Smash Brothers X-treme

What is Street Fighter?

Street Fighter is one of the oldest fighting games in existance. The first game released in 1987 and it is still being played competitively to this day. It is published by Capcom. The aim of the game is to reduce the opponent's hit points to 0. Street Fighter has been one of both the most popular and most influential fighting games.

How to Play

There are 5 types of atacks: light, medium, heavy, grabs, and specials. Light attacks are useful for when you're very close to your opponent and you need a quick attack. Medium attacks are useful for when you want to start a combo. Heavy attacks are most useful for punishing your opponent when they miss an attack. Grabs are useful to attack a blocking opponent. Specials are useful to attack from far away. You can also block and parry to avoid damage. A parry is harder to execute, but has better results.
