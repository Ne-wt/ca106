Player Positions

This is the player positions section. In this section there will be information on the five positions, their roles as well as some NBA players play in each position.

Point Guard

The point guard is commonly referred to the coach on the court because their role is to lead the team's offense. They are positioned at the top of the three point line. They are usually the best dribbler and passer on the team.

Stephen Curry, Golden State Warriors

A player that is highly ranked in this position is Stephen Curry. He is known for his ability insane ability to score shots from long distances.

Shooting Guard

The player who plays this position is usually the team's best shooter. They are positioned on either side of the three point line. A shooting guard should be able to dribble well as well as shooting the ball from long distances.

James Harden, Brooklyn Nets

For the current NBA season, James Harden is ranked the highest in the shooting guard position. He is known for his ability to mesmerize opponents with his dribbling ability and score the ball from anywhere.

Small Forward

The small forward plays against opponents of all sizes, be it big or small. They move around the full width of the basketball court with the ability to put the ball in the basket from long or short distances.

Lebron James, LA Lakers

Lebron James is this years highest ranked player for the small forward position. Lebron James is argued to be one of the best to ever touch a basketball. In his 18th NBA season, at 36 years of age, he is still showing the same 'freakish' athleticism from when he first joined the league in 2003.

Power Forward

The power forward position is similar to the center position in the way that they both play under the basket with the role of scoring inside the paint and catching rebounds. The only difference is that power forwards would take more opportunities to take jump shots.

Giannis Antetokounmpo, Milwaukee Bucks

Giannis Antetokounmpo has a body built to play the power forward position. He is freakish long body proportions that gives him the upper hand against his opponents. Awarding the fitting nickname, "The Greek Freak".

Center

The center is usually the tallest player on the team. They are positioned below the basket. Their main role is to score points under the basket and catch rebounds on both sides of the court.

Nikola Jokic

Nikola Jokic, nicknamed the Joker, won the MVP award for the NBA 2020/21 season. He is known for his court vision and passing ability despite being a center.
