Rules in basketball

This section is the rules section. In this section we will go through 7 of the most basic rules in basketball.

No. of Players

During the game, there should be a total of 10 players on the court at all times. This means that there should be 5 players on each team playing against each other throughout the duration of the game.

Substitutions

When replacing a player on the court with a player from the team's bench, it must only happen during a 'dead ball' situation. A dead ball situation is when the ball is not in play, e.g. free throws, timeouts, or after an out of bounds call.

Traveling

In order for a player who is in possession of the basketball they must dribble the ball. If it happens that a player takes more than two steps without dribbling the ball, the play will be stopped and possession of the ball will be given to the opposing team.

Double Dribble

When a player comes into possession of the ball they will only have one chance at dribbling the ball. Once they pick up the basketball they will either have to pass or shoot the ball. If the player starts dribbling again, the play will be stopped and possession of the all will be given to the opposing team.

Out of Bounds

On a basketball court, there are lines which indicate the area which players who have the ball in hand are to stay within. If the player in possessions of the ball steps outside the line, it will result in a turnover and the ball will be given to the opposing team.

Fouls

In basketball, players are meant to play without unnecessary physical contact. If any player uses physical contact on another player for whatever reason, the game will be stopped and the player will be given a foul. If an individual player manages to collect 5 fouls in a game, they will be fouled out and not be able to play for the remainder of game.

Points

It is possible to score either 1, 2 or 3 points in basketball. A single point will be awarded to a team that successfully scores a free throw. Two points will be given to the team that scores within the three point line. Three points will be awarded to the team that scores beyond the three point line.
