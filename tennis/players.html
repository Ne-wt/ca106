She first competed at proffessional tennis at the age of 14 and she also competed against Karsten Braash at the 1998 Australian Open. Williams won the first title of 2002 in Arizona defeating Jennifer Capriati in the final and she won Miami Masters defeating the worlds top 3 ranked players at one tournament.

Roger Federer was born on the 8th of August 1981 and he is ranked 16th in the world by the Association of Tennis professionals. He has won 20 Grand Slams in singles and been no.1 in ATP rankings. He played his first math in 1996 at 14 years of age where he won both boys singles in Labadze. During 2004 he won three Grand slam singles in a row and he became the first to do so since Mats Wilander. In 2008 at the olympic games he and Stan Wawrinka won gold medal's in doubles. He lost the 2016 of Stuttgart and Halle due to knee problems.
