Famous Players

From Cristiano Ronaldo to Diego Maradona

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is arguably the most famous football player still playing. The Poruguese has scored over 800 goals throughout his playing career, making him the highest goalscorer still playing. He was won many awards throughout his career, including:

  • 5x Ballon D'or
  • 5x Champions League
  • 3x Premier League
  • 19x Top Scorer
You could fill an entire page on his accolades because he has won many more. Although Messi has a better goal per game ratio, I believe that Ronaldo is the GOAT because he has proven himself in many leagues and the team was not built around him, unlike Messi. Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is regarded by many as the greatest footballer to have ever graced the beautiful game. The Argentinian has an amazing record of 756 goals and 318 assists in 948 appearances. Messi and Ronaldo are constantly compared to eachother and it is always a heated debate when asking who is better. Messi's career accolades include:

  • 7x Ballon D'or
  • 4x Champions League
  • 10x La Liga
  • 22x Top Scorer

Diego Maradona

Lionel Messi was called the next Maradona when he started out. Maradona is hailed by Argentinians as one of their all-time greats. He was most prevalent in the 80s, having success in the World Cups during that time. One of his most well-known goals is his 'Hand of God' against England in 1986. This was when he scored a goal with his hand and it was still ruled as a goal. If this was during the present VAR would have ruled the goal out. Here are Maradona's stats:

  • 1x World Cup
  • 1x Europa League
  • 2x Serie A
