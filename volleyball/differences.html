Beach Volleyball

Volleyball played on a sandy court.

Indoor Volleyball

Not neccessarily indoor, but uses a laminated or solid court.

Volleyball differences are not immediately apparent to the unintiated, so we'll go through 4 common ones!

  1. The ball used in each game differs. Indoor volleyballs are made of leather and are heavier than outdoor ones. They're designed to allow powerful strikes and make the game one of endurance. Beach volleyballs are lighter and allow them to 'float'.
  2. The amount of players differ! Beach volleyball is played with two per side, and indoor volleyball is played with six per side.
  3. The court sizes differ! Outdoor volleyball is played on a smaller court, and beach volleyball is played on a smaller court. This is because in the sand mobility is drastically reduced.
  4. Footwear differs drastically. Outdoor they don't wear shoes!
