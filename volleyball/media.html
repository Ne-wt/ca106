X-treme Volleyball.

Volleyball's always been a popular sport since it's creation in the early 20th century, and it's still one of the most popular sports in the world.
However, volleyball's popularity has definitely been influenced by 21st century media and TV-shows that have enticed younger people to play the sport.

One such example is 'The Optimists', a 2013 movie featuring an elderly volleyball team who play their first match after 30 years of practice.


Another example is the popular animated show, 'HAIKYU!!'


Haikyu!! is a Japanese anime series that features a volleyball player named Shōyō Hinata, who is deemed too short by his peers to play the game.

Both are highly acclaimed, and may be a reason for its continous popularity.
