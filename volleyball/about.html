What is Volleyball?

Want to learn about volleyball?
Well, You've come to the right place.
X-treme have been pushing new bounds within the volleyball scene since 1950.

Volleyball is a team based sport that can be played on the court or on the sands of your favourite local beach.
Volleyball is played on a rectangular field with a net. The objective of the sport is to score points by striking the ball into the opposing team's court space.

Volleyball has a lot of rules that can change from court to court or beach to beach, but there are some rules that are always the same.

Here's some examples of those rules:
  • Attempting to score without first getting the ball over the net,
    such as by throwing it under and landing it in the opponents court.
  • Having the ball go out of the court without an opponents interferance
  • Catching the ball or throwing it. You can't hold it at all!
  • Two consecutive hits by the same player.
  • Four consecutive hits by the same team.
  • Net foul aka touching the net during play. You can touch the bottom but not the top!
  • Foul by having your foot outside the court when serving.
